About Me

Coaching, tutoring, and a love for clear thinking

I'm a Strategic Management student at Brigham Young University's Marriott School of Business, and most of what I do outside the classroom traces back to one thing: helping people communicate under pressure. I scored a 36 on the ACT and graduated as a National Merit Finalist, and I've spent the years since turning that into a way of helping other students get there too.

I coach Lincoln-Douglas debate for National Speech and Debate, where I've helped prepare state champion teams for national-level competition. The same instinct that makes debate coaching work, breaking a complicated problem into a structure someone can actually act on, is what drew me to strategic management and data analysis. I also currently teach at the Missionary Training Center, where I adjust instruction daily for different learning speeds and personalities, and I tutor ACT and AP students one-on-one.

On the technical side, I work with SQL, VBA, and Excel, and I'm building proficiency in Brazilian Portuguese for business contexts. I'm also currently working remotely as an AI training expert, testing and evaluating AI models. You can see how all of this fits together on my résumé.
